Swap Operations for Positions

Long positions are charged with the interbank rate plus a mark-up, while short positions receive the rate minus a mark-up. Swaps are processed daily at 00:00 (GMT+2, DST may apply), with triple swaps charged from Wednesday to Thursday.

CFD Margin Calculation

The margin for CFDs is determined as: Lots X Contract Size X Opening Price X Margin Percentage. This method is independent of the leverage in your trading account.

Understanding Equity Indices

Stock indices, like the S&P 500 or Nikkei 225, measure the value of a group of stocks representing specific markets or economies. Calculated via price or capitalization weighting, they serve as indicators of market performance.

Trading Equity Indices at ApexTrade

Equity indices reflect sector-specific or national economic trends. Trade indices like Dow Jones (US30) or CAC 40 (FRA40) to diversify and leverage market movements with real-time updates and precise calculations.
