How to Access ApexTrade on MT5

Step 1:

Download the App

  • Open Google Play Store on your Android device or download MetaTrader here
  • Search for MetaTrader 5 in the search bar
  • Tap on the MetaTrader 5 icon and install the app
Step 2:

Log in or Create an Account

  • Launch the app and choose Login with Existing Account or Open a Demo Account
  • In the search bar, type ApexTrade
  • Select ApexTrade-Demo for a demo account or ApexTrade-Real2 for a live account
Step 3:

Start Trading

  • Enter your login credentials and password
  • Begin trading on your Android/iOS device with ease!
